About

ART+COM Technologies offers technology and competence for media systems.

Development and operation of innovative software and hardware products for interactive multimedia applications are among the core competences of ART+COM Technologies. The company possesses extensive experience in the design and implementation of networked multimedia systems, audio-visual 3D real time simulations and multi-touch solutions.
