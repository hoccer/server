FAQ

Q: Why does Hoccer need access to my personal information like contacts or browser history?

A: With Hoccer you can share your contacts or bookmarks. Thats why Hoccer need access to it.


Q: What is the max. range to hocc something?

A: We ensure you can hocc within 100 meter.


Q: Can I hocc files between Android© and iPhone©?

A: Yes, Hoccer has been developed to work with both platforms.


Q: What happens to the tap gesture?

A: We removed this gesture from Hoccer. The one-to-one feature behind it will come back with a new gesture soon.


Q: How can I hocc to one person only?

A: Everyone who does a catch in time of your throw (and is in range) can hocc the data.


Q: Why can I not see Hoccer in the android market?

A: If you have an Android version which is not supported by Hoccer you can't see the App in market. That's a
     definition by Google.


Q: Will Hoccer be able to throw other files like .zip or .pdf?

A: Yes it will. We are working on and hope we can release this feature soon.


Q: Why I don't see my caught contacts on Android 2.x ?

A: The API for contacts changed in 2.0. We are working for support.


Q: Why does hoccer not work correctly on HTC Hero© or Tattoo© ?

A: We fixed some bugs on these devices. We hope Hoccer will work properly on them now.


Q: How does Hoccer transport the files from phone to phone?

A: At first there is no Bluetooth! Hoccer need an internet connection. If you do a throw the phone sends
     the file attached with a location to a server. When another does a catch the location will also send to the server.
     If both are in range the file will be send to the receiver.


Q: How locate Hoccer my position?

A: At first Hoccer try to match one access point in your range. If both peers see the one and equal AP
      you doesn't need a GPS connection to hocc files. Second Android© try to get a location via GPS. Otherwise
      it try to locate you by check your 'network location', but keep in mind this must not be very accurate.


Q: How can I hocc between my phone and my computer?

A: On this site you can throw and catch files. You just have to locate your position on the map.


Q: How can I hocc my files without using a gestures?

A: Not on the phone. Only on this site you can throw and catch without to make a gesture.
